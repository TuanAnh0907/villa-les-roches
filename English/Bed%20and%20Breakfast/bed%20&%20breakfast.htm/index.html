Bedrooms leading directly onto the Lime Tree Terrace

The three B&B bedrooms at Les Roches are at the Lower Lime Tree Terrace level, which means just a quick nip outside to sunbathe on the terrace! The rooms have been stylishly decorated by your hostess, Christine Cooper, a former dress designer and each room has its own private bathroom.

The Myrtille Room (Wild Blueberry Room)

Bedroom  Spacious and filled with morning sunlight when you wake, the Myrtille room has a very wide double bed, (180 cms) that can be split into 2 twins if required, (please specify when you book).
Bathroom: The Myrtille bathroom is en suite and has a screened bath with shower inside the bath.

The Chanterelle Room (Wild Mushroom Room)

Bedroom Serenely romantic, the Chanterelle room has a 140 cm wide double bed draped with a voile canopy inspired by a medieval throne!
Bathroom: The Chanterelle room has an en suite bathroom that was Villa les Roche's original Art Déco bathroom. It has, of course, been updated to include a shower cabinet and a toilet but it still contains its period style bath, washbasin and original floor tiles.

The Sureau Room (Elderflower Room)

BedroomFresh as springtime and filled with morning sunlight, this room has a pretty 140 cm wide wrought-iron double bed.
Bathroom:    The private Sureau bathroom is the only bathroom that is not en suite with the bedroom, but it is only 5 steps away, along the corridor and is reserved exclusively for Sureau room guests. It is prettily decorated with hand stencilling and contains a bath (shower inside), toilet and washbasin.

Breakfast at Villa les Roches

Breakfast at Villa les Roches is, of course, included in the bed & breakfast room price. It is served in the convivial atmosphere of a large, elegant dining room. On offer are: fruit starter, fruit juice, cereals, yogurts, a variety of breads &  pastries, toast, jams,  cheeses, etc.

 

 

 

Two Options for your Evening Meal:
  • You could order a "Just Like Home" meal at Villa les Roches, for the night of your arrival. This simple meal is only available by advance order in the months of April, May and June.
  • You could choose from a range of restaurants in and around Langogne.

 

"Just Like Home" Evening Meal

(Only available by advance order during the months of April, May and June.)

We regret we do not offer table d'hôte meals with a changing menu, but we do make available this simple meal as a service to those guests arriving from far away or those who are on foot and for whom it would be difficult to go into Langogne, (1.5 km. from the house) for a restaurant meal. It is a semi-buffet meal, where you help yourself.

Please book this meal at the same time as making your room reservation.to avoid disappointment.

 

Menu for the "Just Like Home" evening meal

Mixed salad - Casserole with rice - Cheese platter - Fruit dessert - Bread - Wine

Price: 19.00 euros per person.
