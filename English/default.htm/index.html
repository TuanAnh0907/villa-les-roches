Elegant B&B and Gîte Apartment  in a  Picturesque Setting

Open Easter till end of September

 

Guests at Villa les Roches may choose to stay in one of three delightful bed and breakfast rooms or spend a week self-catering in our new gîte - the beautiful Pine Tops Apartment. This imposing house is located in a peaceful setting, down a private lane on the outskirts of Langogne, a small mountain town in Lozère, South of France. It is built high on the rocky slopes of an extraordinarily picturesque river gorge and commands panoramic views over the pine-clad mid-mountains all around. South facing on three floors, this lovely house is filled with sun from dawn to dusk.

 

Two glass-enclosed verandas provide exceptional vistas over the gorge, especially from the Pine Tops Apartment Gîte.

 

Two terraces surround the house where you can relax in a chaise longue or you may prefer to wander through rocky woods down to our own shady stretch of the river, which meanders towards a waterfall. (You’ll need your wellies!).

 

 

 

The surrounding Lozèrian countryside remains wild and unspoilt and is punctuated by delightful river gorges and rivulets, providing a landscape that is perfect for walking, painting, fishing and pure escapism! Maps and walking routes translated into English can be loaned during your stay.

 

The town of Langogne is on the borders of three departments - Lozère, Ardèche and Haute Loire, each with its own distinct countryside. Villa les Roches is the perfect base from which to explore this varied area of the South of France.
