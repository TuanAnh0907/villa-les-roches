How to get to Villa Les Roches

Click on thumbnail map below to see location of Villa les Roches on southern outskirts of Langogne.

See Michelin map of Langogne.

By car:

Langogne is situated on the N88, north of Mende, Lozère, (department 48) and south of Le Puy en Velay, (department 43).

By air from Great Britain:

Ryanair to Rodez is an easy drive to Les Roches.

Easy Jet to Lyon is another alternative.

Ryanair to Nîmes, or Montpellier are also possible.

By train:

Langogne is on the Paris - Nîmes railway line. (6 - 7 hours from Paris)

Consult SNCF timetable
