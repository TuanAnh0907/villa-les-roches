Short Stay Tax 2017 (Taxe de Séjour)

Bed & Breakfast

A short-stay tax of 0.50 euros per night, per person (over 13 yrs) will be added to your bill from 15th June to 15th September. The local council uses this tax for the development and promotion of tourism in the Langogne neighbourhood.

Pine Tops Apartment

A short-stay tax of 0.80 euros per night, per person (over 13 yrs) will be added to your bill from 15th June to 15th September. The local council uses this tax for the development and promotion of tourism in the Langogne neighbourhood.

 

 

Booking

Please contact us, as below, preferably by email, to check availability. We will ask you for a deposit, (1/2 of total for one or two nights' B&B and 1/3rd for longer B&B stays and for Pine Tops Apartment bookings), payable at the time of booking in euros. The balance of the stay will be due on arrival.

B&B Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel your booking, you may do so up to 4 weeks before your booked date and your deposit will be refunded in full. If you give us less than 4 weeks' notice of cancellation, we can only refund your deposit if we are able to relet the accommodation.

 

Pine Tops Apartment Cancellation Policy

If you need to cancel your booking, you may do so up to 6 weeks before your booked date and your deposit will be refunded in full. If you give us less than 6 weeks' notice, we can only refund your deposit if we are able to relet the accommodation.
